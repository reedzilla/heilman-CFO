Heilman's 21-Point Inspection

Every visit, your vehicle gets our full 21-point inspection — and we review the results with you when the work is done. Here's exactly what our technicians check:

1 Verify factory scheduled maintenance is up to date
2 Test operation of all exterior lights
3 Inspect windshield washer operation and fluid level
4 Inspect operation of A/C & heating system
5 Check engine oil & filter / window sticker
6 Check transmission fluid level & condition
7 Check power steering fluid
8 Check engine coolant level & condition
9 Inspect belts for cracking, fraying, slippage & wear
10 Check air filter
11 Load test battery & inspect battery cables
12 Inspect condition of radiator & heating hoses
13 Visually inspect undercarriage for damage or leaks
14 Inspect tires (incl. spare) for inflation & wear
15 Inspect CV boots & axles
16 Inspect condition of exhaust system
17 Inspect engine & transmission mounts
18 Inspect front & rear brakes / test emergency brake
19 Inspect brake / clutch hydraulic system
20 Inspect ignition components & fuel filter
21 Visual check of shock absorbers or struts
